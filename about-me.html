A Snippet of My Story

As a Digital Business student at Universitas Padjadjaran, I've developed a strong foundation in business consulting and strategic analysis. With a current GPA of 3.73/4.00, I combine academic excellence with practical experience in solving real-world business challenges.

My consulting journey has been particularly enriching at Nanno Consulting Firm, where I've contributed to diverse projects across water management, banking, and shipping sectors. Notable projects include the Performance Evaluation Study for BUMD Kota Bandung (2023), Feasibility Analysis for PDAM Tirtawening's Business Sub-units (2024), and the Development Study for PT ASDP's Smart Ferizy Lounge vessels (2024).

At LMFEB Unpad, I've further honed my consulting skills by working on strategic initiatives for various organizations. Key projects included developing programs for MSMEs and cooperatives, optimizing operations for Antam's gold boutiques, and creating sustainability strategies for Perumda Pasar Juara Kota Bandung.

Beyond consulting, I'm passionate about education and technology. As a Computer Programming Teaching Assistant at Universitas Padjadjaran, I've helped 99 Digital Business students master Python programming, covering essential concepts and practical applications using libraries like NumPy and Matplotlib.

I'm also proud to have founded the TWG Community, where I led a team of 8 to develop a comprehensive program that equipped 27 participants with professional skills through mentoring, classes, and practical case studies.

My commitment to excellence has been recognized through various achievements, including winning first place in the DigCofest Business Case Competition 2024, securing second place in the 8th SEAA-PEREGRINE International Case Competition 2024, and being a finalist in multiple prestigious business competitions.

I believe in the power of combining analytical thinking with practical implementation, whether it's developing business strategies, managing social media campaigns, or leading community initiatives. My goal is to continue making meaningful contributions to organizations while fostering growth and innovation in the business world.
